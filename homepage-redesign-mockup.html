Questions

Frequently Asked

How does virtual therapy work?
Sessions are conducted through a secure, PIPEDA-compliant video platform. You can access therapy from anywhere in Ontario with a stable internet connection. Research shows virtual therapy is just as effective as in-person for most concerns.
Does insurance cover therapy with a Registered Psychotherapist?
Most extended health plans in Ontario cover sessions with Registered Psychotherapists (CRPO). Coverage typically ranges from $500-2000 annually. I provide detailed receipts for easy insurance reimbursement.
How quickly can I start therapy?
After your initial contact, I typically schedule a free 15-minute consultation within 2-3 days. If we're a good fit, first sessions are usually available within one week. Evening and weekend appointments are available.
What's the difference between ACT and CBT?
CBT focuses on identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns. ACT focuses on accepting difficult thoughts while committing to values-based action. I integrate both approaches based on what works best for each client's specific situation.
What if I've tried therapy before and it didn't work?
Many clients have had underwhelming therapy experiences. My approach is different: structured, goal-oriented, and focused on building practical skills rather than open-ended processing. We track progress with validated measures and adjust the approach if something isn't working.

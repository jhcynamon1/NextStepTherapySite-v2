Understanding Workplace Burnout

When Work Takes Over Everything

Burnout isn't just being tired - it's emotional exhaustion, cynicism, and feeling ineffective at work. If Sunday nights fill you with dread or you can't remember the last time you felt excited about your job, you're not alone and you don't have to stay stuck.

Beyond "Just Take a Vacation"

Real workplace stress requires more than time off. Using Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and proven stress management techniques, we'll develop sustainable strategies that work within your actual work environment - not just idealized scenarios.

Your Career, Your Values

We'll explore what actually matters to you in work and life, then build practical skills for maintaining those values even in demanding workplaces. This isn't about quitting everything - it's about finding sustainability in what you do.

The Process

How Workplace Stress Therapy Works

A clear, structured approach that respects your time and focuses on real-world results you can implement immediately.

1

Initial Assessment

We start with a comprehensive look at your specific workplace challenges, stress patterns, and what you want to change. Free 15-minute consultation to ensure we're a good fit.

2

Strategy Development

Together, we develop practical tools tailored to your actual work environment - not theoretical scenarios. Boundary scripts, stress management techniques, and communication strategies that work with your personality.

3

Implementation & Support

Practice new approaches in real-time. Troubleshoot challenges as they arise. Build confidence in handling workplace stress without it overwhelming your entire life.

4

Long-term Sustainability

Develop systems that prevent future burnout. Learn to recognize early warning signs. Create a sustainable relationship with work that aligns with your actual values and life goals.

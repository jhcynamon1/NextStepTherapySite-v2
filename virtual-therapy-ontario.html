Frequently Asked Questions

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person therapy?
Yes, extensive research shows that virtual therapy is equally effective as in-person therapy for most mental health concerns, including anxiety, depression, and stress management.
Is virtual therapy covered by insurance in Ontario?
Yes, most extended health insurance plans in Ontario cover virtual therapy sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. We provide direct billing for many insurance providers.
What technology do I need for virtual therapy?
You only need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone) and a stable internet connection. We use a secure, easy-to-use platform that doesn't require downloads.
How do I book a virtual therapy session?
Simply click the "Book Now" button or call 647-370-5507. We offer a free 15-minute consultation to discuss your needs and answer any questions.
Can I access virtual therapy from anywhere in Ontario?
Yes! As a CRPO-registered psychotherapist, I can provide virtual therapy services to anyone located in Ontario, regardless of your city or region.

Men's Mental Health Therapy in London, Ontario

"I don't know why I'm here. I've never done this therapy thing before." That's how most of my male clients in London start our first session. There's something about being a man in Southwestern Ontario—whether you're working at the hospital, trying to make it in tech, or dealing with layoffs in traditional industries—that makes asking for help feel like failure. But here's what I've learned: the strongest thing you can do is recognize when you need support.

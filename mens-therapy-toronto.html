Frequently Asked Questions About Men's Therapy in Toronto

How much does men's therapy cost in Toronto?

Men's therapy in Toronto typically costs $140-$195 per 50-minute session. At Next Step Therapy, sessions are $175. Many extended health insurance plans cover a portion of this cost ($350-$500 annually).

Does OHIP cover men's mental health therapy?

No, OHIP does not cover psychotherapy services in Ontario. However, most employer extended health benefits include mental health coverage. Check your benefits package or contact your provider for specifics.

What insurance plans cover therapy sessions?

Most major insurance providers cover psychotherapy: Green Shield Canada, Manulife, Sun Life, Great-West Life, and others. Coverage typically ranges from $350-$500 annually. I provide receipts for all sessions for insurance claims.

What happens in a first men's therapy session?

The first session focuses on understanding your challenges (workplace stress, anxiety, relationship issues), discussing your goals, and creating a practical plan. It's conversational, confidential, and judgment-free. You don't need to "open up" about everything immediately.

How long does men's therapy typically take?

Most men work with me for 8-10 sessions, seeing improvements in stress management and coping strategies. Some continue longer for ongoing support. We'll discuss your timeline and goals in our first session.

What's different about therapy specifically for men?

Men's therapy takes a solution-focused, practical approach rather than just "talking about feelings." We identify specific problems (workplace stress, relationship challenges) and develop actionable strategies. It's more like strategic problem-solving than emotional processing.

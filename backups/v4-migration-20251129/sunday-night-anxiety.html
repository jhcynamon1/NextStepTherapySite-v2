Does Your Sunday Look Like This?

It starts around 3 PM. The shadow of Monday morning creeps in, stealing the last hours of your weekend.

The 4 PM Pit

You feel a physical drop in your stomach as the afternoon fades. This weekend anxiety makes the relaxation of Saturday feel like a distant memory.

The Mental Rehearsal

You can't stop running through tomorrow's to-do list, emails, and meetings. You're "at work" before you've even left your couch.

The Sleep Sabotage

You're exhausted, but you can't sleep. You stare at the ceiling, calculating how many hours of rest you'll get if you fall asleep right now.

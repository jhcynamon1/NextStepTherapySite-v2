Questions

Frequently asked

How does virtual therapy work?
Sessions happen via secure, PIPEDA-compliant video. All you need is a private space and stable internet. Research shows virtual therapy is just as effective as in-person for anxiety, depression, and stress.
Does insurance cover this?
Most extended health plans cover Registered Psychotherapists. Coverage typically ranges from $500-2000 annually. I provide detailed receipts for easy reimbursement.
How quickly can I start?
Consultations are typically available within 2-3 days. First sessions usually within a week. Same-week appointments available for urgent concerns. Evening and weekend slots offered.
What if therapy hasn't worked for me before?
Many clients have had underwhelming experiences. My approach is different: structured, goal-oriented, and focused on practical skills rather than open-ended processing. We track progress with validated measures and adjust if something isn't working.
What's the difference between ACT and CBT?
CBT focuses on identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns. ACT focuses on accepting difficult thoughts while committing to values-based action. I integrate both based on what works best for your specific situation.

Understanding the Student Mental Health Crisis

Ontario's post-secondary students face unprecedented mental health challenges. With 65% of students reporting overwhelming anxiety and 46% experiencing depression symptoms, professional support has never been more critical. Next Step Therapy specializes in helping students navigate these challenges with evidence-based, accessible virtual therapy.

Our therapists understand the unique pressures of academic life - from exam anxiety and imposter syndrome to social isolation and financial stress. We provide practical, student-focused therapy that fits your schedule and budget, covered by most student health plans.

Student Mental Health Market Opportunity

Ontario has over 700,000 post-secondary students actively seeking mental health support. With campus counselling waitlists extending months, there's significant demand for qualified therapists specializing in student mental health.

Market insights:

  • Peak demand: September-April academic year

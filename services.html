Evidence-Based Mental Health Support

At Next Step Therapy, we provide professional mental health support grounded in research and tailored to your unique needs. Our approach combines evidence-based therapeutic methods with genuine understanding and practical strategies you can use in daily life.

Whether you're dealing with anxiety that keeps you up at night, depression that makes everything feel heavy, workplace stress that follows you home, or major life transitions that feel overwhelming, we're here to help you navigate these challenges.

Virtual therapy removes barriers to getting help. No commute, no waiting rooms, just professional support from wherever you feel most comfortable.

Our Therapeutic Approach

Every person's journey is unique, which is why we don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. Our approach integrates multiple evidence-based modalities to create a personalized treatment plan that works for you.

We draw from Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), helping you develop psychological flexibility and live according to your values even when facing difficult emotions. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques help identify and shift unhelpful thought patterns. Mindfulness practices build awareness and reduce reactivity to stress.

But beyond techniques and theories, we believe in the power of genuine human connection. Therapy is a collaborative process where you're the expert on your life, and we provide professional guidance, tools, and support to help you create meaningful change.

Virtual Therapy Throughout Ontario

Living in Ontario means dealing with long commutes, busy schedules, and sometimes limited access to mental health services in your area. Virtual therapy eliminates these barriers, making professional support accessible no matter where you are in the province.

From Toronto to Thunder Bay, Ottawa to Windsor, we provide the same quality of care through secure video sessions. You can attend therapy from your home, office, or any private space where you feel comfortable.

Virtual sessions are just as effective as in-person therapy, with the added benefits of:

  • No commute time or parking stress
  • Greater scheduling flexibility
  • Access from anywhere in Ontario
  • Comfort of your chosen environment
  • Continuity of care when you travel

Insurance Coverage & Fees

We believe mental health support should be accessible. As a Registered Psychotherapist (CRPO #10979), our services are covered by most extended health insurance plans in Ontario.

We provide official receipts for all sessions that you can submit to your insurance provider. Many plans cover psychotherapy services, and we're happy to help you understand your coverage during your free consultation.

For those without insurance, we offer transparent pricing and can discuss options to make therapy affordable. Your mental health is an investment in your overall well-being and quality of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

If you're struggling with emotions, relationships, or life challenges that feel overwhelming or persistent, therapy can help. Our free consultation lets you explore whether our approach fits your needs without any commitment.

What can I expect in the first session?

The first session focuses on understanding your concerns and goals. We'll discuss what brings you to therapy, explore your history briefly, and begin developing a plan that addresses your specific needs.

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration varies based on individual needs and goals. Some people find relief in a few sessions, while others benefit from longer-term support. We'll regularly review your progress and adjust our approach as needed.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person?

Research shows that virtual therapy is just as effective as in-person sessions for most mental health concerns. Many clients actually prefer the convenience and comfort of attending sessions from their own space.

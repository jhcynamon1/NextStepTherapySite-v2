Types of Depression We Treat in Windsor, Ontario

Major Depressive Disorder

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, and difficulty functioning in daily life.

Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD)

Depression that follows a seasonal pattern, common during Windsor's grey winter months.

Persistent Depressive Disorder

Chronic, low-grade depression that lasts for two years or more (formerly Dysthymia).

Situational Depression

Depressive symptoms triggered by specific life events like job loss, divorce, or bereavement.

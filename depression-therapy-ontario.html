Understanding Depression Through an ACT Lens

Depression therapy traditionally focuses on eliminating symptoms, but Acceptance and Commitment Therapy takes a different approach. Rather than fighting against difficult emotions, we develop skills to navigate them while pursuing what matters most to you.

Common experiences with depression include:

  • Persistent low mood and lack of motivation
  • Loss of interest in previously enjoyable activities
  • Difficulty concentrating and making decisions
  • Sleep disturbances and fatigue
  • Feelings of worthlessness or excessive guilt
  • Social withdrawal and isolation

Evidence-Based Depression Treatment Approach

Our depression counseling utilizes Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), which research shows to be highly effective for mood difficulties. This approach helps you develop psychological flexibility and reconnect with your values.

Key therapeutic focuses include:

  • Acceptance of difficult emotions without being controlled by them
  • Mindfulness skills for present-moment awareness
  • Values clarification and committed action
  • Cognitive defusion from unhelpful thought patterns
  • Behavioral activation aligned with personal values
  • Building meaningful connections and relationships

Virtual Depression Therapy Across Ontario

Beyond Symptom Management

Traditional depression treatment often focuses solely on reducing symptoms. Our approach recognizes that a meaningful life includes the full range of human emotions, including sadness and disappointment. We work on building resilience and flexibility rather than emotional avoidance.

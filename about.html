Meet Jesse Cynamon, MPsy, RP

As a Registered Psychotherapist with sports operations professional, Jesse brings a unique perspective to helping high-performers navigate anxiety and stress. Having worked in high-pressure environments himself, Jesse understands the unique challenges faced by ambitious professionals. His approach combines clinical expertise with real-world experience in performance settings, offering both understanding and practical strategies for those seeking to excel while maintaining wellbeing.

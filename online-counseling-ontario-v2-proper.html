Frequently Asked Questions About Online Counseling

Is online counseling as effective as in-person therapy?
Research consistently shows online counseling is equally effective as in-person therapy for most mental health concerns. Virtual sessions offer additional benefits including comfort of your own space, no travel time, enhanced privacy, and access to specialized therapists throughout Ontario.
What technology do I need for online counseling sessions?
You need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with camera and microphone, plus reliable internet connection. Our platform is web-based requiring no downloads. We provide technical support and phone backup options if needed for virtual therapy sessions.
Is online counseling covered by insurance in Ontario?
Yes, most extended health plans cover online counseling with Registered Psychotherapists identical to in-person sessions. We provide detailed receipts with appropriate codes for insurance reimbursement. Sessions are $175 per 50-minute session.
How do you ensure privacy and confidentiality in online sessions?
We use HIPAA-compliant platforms with end-to-end encryption ensuring complete confidentiality. All online therapy sessions maintain identical privacy standards to in-person therapy, with additional digital security measures for virtual delivery.
What if I have technical problems during a session?
We provide technical support and backup protocols including phone sessions. Most technical issues are easily resolved quickly. Our platform is designed for reliability, and we test connections before first virtual therapy sessions.
Can online counseling help with serious mental health conditions?
Online counseling effectively addresses most mental health concerns including depression, anxiety, trauma, and relationship issues. For conditions requiring medication management, we coordinate with healthcare providers while providing therapeutic support.
What areas of Ontario do you serve with online counseling?
Our virtual therapy services are available throughout Ontario, including rural and remote areas with internet access. Online delivery eliminates geographic barriers, providing professional support to communities with limited local resources.
How do I prepare for my first online counseling session?
Choose a private, comfortable space with good internet connection. Test your audio/video beforehand. Consider your therapy goals and questions. We'll provide technical orientation and help you optimize your online therapy experience.

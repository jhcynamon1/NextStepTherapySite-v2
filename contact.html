What to Expect

During Your Free Consultation

Your 15-minute consultation is an opportunity to:

  • Share briefly what brings you to therapy
  • Ask questions about the therapeutic process
  • Learn about our approach and how it might help
  • Discuss scheduling and practical considerations
  • Determine if we're a good fit to work together

This consultation is completely free with no obligation to continue. It's simply a chance for us to connect and for you to make an informed decision about your mental health care.

Your First Full Session

If you decide to move forward after the consultation, your first full session will focus on:

  • Understanding your history and current concerns in more depth
  • Identifying your goals for therapy
  • Beginning to develop a treatment plan tailored to your needs
  • Starting to build the therapeutic relationship

Insurance & Payment Options

Insurance Coverage

As a Registered Psychotherapist (CRPO #10979), our services are covered by most extended health insurance plans in Ontario. We provide official receipts for all sessions that clearly state:

  • Provider name and CRPO registration number
  • Date and duration of service
  • Service provided (psychotherapy)
  • Amount paid

Check with your insurance provider about your specific coverage for "Psychotherapy" or "Registered Psychotherapist" services. Many plans cover between $500-$3000 annually.

Payment Methods

We accept:

  • E-transfer (preferred)
  • Credit card (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Direct billing (select insurance providers)

Fees

Our session fees are competitive and transparent. We'll discuss the exact fee structure during your free consultation. For those without insurance coverage, please ask about available options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We often have same-week availability for new clients. During your consultation call, we'll find a time that works for your schedule.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that life happens. We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling when possible. We'll discuss our full policy during your consultation.

Is everything confidential?

Yes, all communications and sessions are strictly confidential within legal and ethical guidelines. We comply with PHIPA standards for protecting your health information.

What technology do I need for virtual sessions?

You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone) and a stable internet connection. We use a secure, PHIPA-compliant video platform that's easy to access.

What if therapy isn't right for me?

The free consultation helps determine if we're a good fit. If we're not the right match, or if you need a different type of support, we'll do our best to provide appropriate referrals.

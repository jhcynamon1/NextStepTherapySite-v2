Mental Health Support for Mississauga Residents

Mississauga's identity as a major business hub brings specific mental health challenges. The corporate environment creates career pressure and work-life balance issues. The city's cultural diversity is a strength, but can also create identity and belonging challenges for newcomers. Common Concerns for Mississauga Residents:

Supporting newcomers to Canada navigating cultural differences, identity questions, and building community connections.

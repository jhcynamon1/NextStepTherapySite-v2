# SEO Framework for Therapy/Healthcare Content

## Topic Clusters for Therapy Content

### Core Topics (Must Cover)
These topics appear on 80%+ of top-ranking therapy pages:

1. **Treatment Approach**
   - Specific modalities (CBT, ACT, psychodynamic, etc.)
   - How the approach works
   - What makes it effective
   - Success rates/timeline

2. **Cost & Insurance**
   - Session pricing
   - Insurance coverage details
   - Payment methods
   - Reimbursement process

3. **Therapist Qualifications**
   - Credentials (CRPO, license #)
   - Education/training
   - Areas of specialization
   - Years of experience

4. **Session Structure**
   - What to expect in first session
   - Typical session length
   - Frequency of sessions
   - Treatment duration

5. **Virtual vs In-Person**
   - Availability of online therapy
   - How virtual sessions work
   - Effectiveness comparison
   - Technology requirements

### Secondary Topics (60-80% Coverage)
Include these to match better performers:

1. **Specific Conditions Treated**
   - Anxiety (types: social, generalized, panic)
   - Depression
   - Workplace stress/burnout
   - Relationship issues
   - Life transitions

2. **Target Populations**
   - Professionals/executives
   - Students/university
   - Men's mental health
   - LGBTQ+ individuals
   - Specific age groups

3. **Treatment Process**
   - Initial assessment
   - Goal setting
   - Progress tracking
   - Ending therapy

4. **Logistics**
   - Booking process
   - Cancellation policy
   - Emergency protocol
   - Contact methods

### Gap Opportunities (0-40% Coverage)
Topics underserved by competitors:

1. **Regulatory Details**
   - Specific CRPO license number
   - Provincial regulations
   - Professional standards
   - Complaint process

2. **Insurance Specifics**
   - Named Ontario insurance providers
   - Typical coverage amounts
   - Direct billing options
   - HSA/FSA details

3. **Success Stories**
   - Specific case examples
   - Patient outcomes (anonymized)
   - Before/after scenarios
   - Testimonials with details

4. **Comparison Content**
   - ACT vs CBT detailed comparison
   - Therapy vs medication
   - Self-help vs professional help
   - Group vs individual therapy

5. **Local Context**
   - City-specific information
   - Local mental health resources
   - Community partnerships
   - Regional demographics

## E-E-A-T Requirements for YMYL Content

### Experience Signals
- First-person perspective ("I help clients...")
- Specific examples from practice
- Years in practice stated
- Case studies (anonymized)

### Expertise Signals
- **Required:**
  - Specific license/credential number
  - Registration body (CRPO, etc.)
  - Educational background
  - Specializations/certifications

- **Recommended:**
  - Published work/research
  - Professional memberships
  - Continuing education
  - Awards/recognition

### Authoritativeness Signals
- **Citations Required:**
  - Government sources (canada.ca, ontario.ca)
  - Professional bodies (CRPO, CMHA)
  - Research institutions (NIMH, etc.)
  - Peer-reviewed studies

- **Links Recommended:**
  - Professional association profiles
  - Published articles/papers
  - Media mentions
  - Speaking engagements

### Trustworthiness Signals
- **Required:**
  - Privacy policy
  - Contact information
  - Physical/virtual address
  - Phone number
  - Email address

- **Recommended:**
  - SSL certificate (https)
  - Professional photos
  - Transparent pricing
  - Clear policies
  - Client testimonials
  - Review schema markup

## Schema Markup Priority

### Tier 1 (Essential)
1. **MedicalWebPage**
   - Signals healthcare content
   - Improves relevance
   - Required for medical SERP features

2. **FAQPage**
   - Enables rich snippets
   - Answers common questions
   - Improves CTR

3. **LocalBusiness / ProfessionalService**
   - Critical for location-based searches
   - Map pack eligibility
   - Local SEO boost

### Tier 2 (High Value)
4. **Review / AggregateRating**
   - Star ratings in SERPs
   - Trust signals
   - CTR improvement

5. **Person**
   - Therapist profile
   - Credential display
   - E-E-A-T boost

### Tier 3 (Nice to Have)
6. **BreadcrumbList**
   - Navigation clarity
   - SERP display
   - UX improvement

7. **HowTo / QAPage**
   - Process explanations
   - Featured snippet potential
   - Engagement

## Local SEO Requirements (Ontario)

### Essential Elements
- City name in title tag
- Ontario mention in first 100 words
- Local schema with address
- CRPO reference (Ontario-specific)
- GeoCoordinates if physical location

### Ontario-Specific Content
- OHIP coverage details
- Ontario insurance providers
- Provincial regulations
- Local mental health resources
- Regional demographics

### City-Specific Content (for location pages)
- University mentions (e.g., Western, McMaster)
- Major employers/industries
- Local transit/accessibility
- Neighborhood details
- City-specific mental health data

## Word Count Guidelines

### Service Pages
- **Minimum:** 1,500 words
- **Target:** 2,500-3,500 words
- **Maximum:** 5,000 words (diminishing returns)

### Blog Posts
- **Minimum:** 1,200 words
- **Target:** 2,000-2,500 words
- **Maximum:** 4,000 words

### Location Pages
- **Minimum:** 800 words
- **Target:** 1,200-1,800 words
- **Maximum:** 2,500 words

## Readability Targets

- **Grade Level:** 7th-9th grade (Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Flesch Reading Ease:** 60-70
- **Sentence Length:** Average 15-20 words
- **Paragraph Length:** 3-5 sentences max

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Minimum Links
- **Service pages:** 5-10 internal links
- **Blog posts:** 3-7 internal links
- **Location pages:** 8-15 internal links

### Link Targets
- Related service pages
- Relevant blog posts
- Location pages
- About/credentials page
- Contact/booking page

## Content Freshness

### Update Frequency
- **Service pages:** Review every 6 months
- **Blog posts:** Update yearly
- **Location pages:** Update every 6 months

### Freshness Signals
- "Last Updated: [Date]"
- "Reviewed by: [Therapist Name], CRPO #[Number]"
- Current year mentions (2025)
- Recent statistics/data
- Current event references

## Competitive Advantages to Leverage

### Unique to You
- Specific CRPO license number
- ACT specialization
- Virtual-first model
- Same-week availability
- Evening/weekend hours
- Ontario-wide service

### Common Gaps in Competitors
- No Review schema (even with testimonials)
- Vague insurance info
- Missing author bios
- No freshness signals
- Generic location content
- No ACT vs CBT comparison

## Red Flags to Avoid

### Content Issues
- Keyword stuffing
- Thin content (<1,000 words)
- Duplicate content
- No unique value
- Outdated information

### Technical Issues
- Missing schema markup
- Broken internal links
- No mobile optimization
- Slow page speed
- Mixed content (http/https)

### E-E-A-T Issues
- No credentials shown
- No author information
- No citations
- Unverified claims
- No contact info
- Missing privacy policy

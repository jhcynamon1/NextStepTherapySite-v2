Understanding Workplace Stress and Burnout

Workplace stress has become epidemic in today's fast-paced professional environment. Burnout therapy addresses the physical, emotional, and mental exhaustion that results from prolonged exposure to emotionally demanding work situations.

Signs you might benefit from workplace anxiety support:

  • Chronic fatigue and exhaustion
  • Difficulty concentrating at work
  • Increased irritability or cynicism
  • Physical symptoms like headaches or sleep issues
  • Feeling overwhelmed by daily tasks
  • Loss of motivation and job satisfaction
  • Anxiety about performance or job security

Evidence-Based Workplace Stress Treatment

Our workplace stress therapy utilizes proven approaches:

  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) - Develop psychological flexibility to handle workplace pressures
  • Cognitive Behavioral strategies - Identify and change unhelpful thought patterns
  • Mindfulness techniques - Build stress resilience and emotional regulation
  • Values-based decision making - Align career choices with personal values
  • Boundary setting skills - Create sustainable work practices

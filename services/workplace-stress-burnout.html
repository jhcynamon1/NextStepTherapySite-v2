Common Questions About Workplace Therapy

Will my employer find out?

Absolutely not. Sessions are completely confidential. Insurance receipts go directly to you, not your employer. Virtual sessions mean no one sees you in a waiting room.

Can you help with toxic workplace situations?

Yes. We'll work on strategies to protect your mental health while navigating difficult dynamics, including how to set boundaries and when it might be time to move on.

What if I'm considering leaving my job?

Career transitions are common discussion topics. We'll explore what's driving the desire to leave, what you're looking for, and how to make decisions aligned with your values.

How do you understand corporate pressure if you're a therapist?

I work with professionals across industries and understand the unique pressures of corporate environments. We'll speak the same language about workplace dynamics.

Can therapy really help with work stress?

Absolutely. We'll develop concrete strategies for managing stress, improving boundaries, and finding sustainable ways to perform without burning out.

When can I schedule sessions?

Evening and weekend appointments are available specifically for working professionals. Virtual sessions mean you can connect from home or a private space.

Common Questions Men Ask

Is therapy just talking about feelings?

Not at all. We focus on practical solutions and strategies. Sometimes we talk about feelings, sometimes we problem-solve, sometimes we just figure out what the hell is going on.

Will anyone know I'm in therapy?

No. Virtual sessions mean no waiting rooms. Everything is confidential. Insurance receipts are provided directly to you - your employer never knows.

How is this different from talking to a friend?

Friends give advice based on their experience. I provide professional strategies without judgment. Plus, you don't have to worry about burdening me or changing our relationship.

What if I don't know what to talk about?

That's normal. I'll guide the conversation. Start with what's bothering you most right now - work stress, relationship issues, or just feeling off. We'll figure it out together.

How long does therapy take?

Some guys need a few sessions to work through a specific issue. Others find ongoing support helpful. There's no mandatory timeline - we work until you feel better.

Do you only work with men?

No, I work with everyone. But I understand the unique pressures men face and have specific approaches that resonate with how many men process challenges.

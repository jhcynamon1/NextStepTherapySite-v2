Common Questions

How does virtual therapy work? +

Virtual therapy sessions are conducted through secure, PIPEDA-compliant video platforms. You can access professional therapy from the comfort of your home anywhere in Ontario. It's just as effective as in-person therapy for most concerns.

Does insurance cover therapy? +

Yes, most extended health plans in Ontario cover sessions with Registered Psychotherapists (CRPO). Coverage typically ranges from $500-2000 annually. I provide detailed receipts for easy reimbursement.

What is Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)? +

ACT is an evidence-based approach that helps you develop psychological flexibility. Rather than fighting difficult thoughts, we learn to accept them while taking action on what truly matters to you.

How quickly can I start? +

I typically schedule free 15-minute consultations within 2-3 days. If we're a good fit, first sessions are usually available within one week.

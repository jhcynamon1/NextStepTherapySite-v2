Why Workplace Stress Hits Harder in Hamilton

Hamilton's diverse economy creates unique pressures. From the high-stakes environment of our hospitals to the demands of our industrial sector, workers here face distinct challenges.

Common Hamilton Stressors We Treat:

  • Healthcare Burnout: Systemic strain affecting staff at Hamilton Health Sciences & St. Joe's.
  • Industrial Stress: The physical and mental toll of shift work and production demands.
  • Commuter Fatigue: The soul-crushing QEW/GO commute to Toronto jobs.
  • High-Functioning Anxiety: Looking successful on the outside while drowning in stress internally.

You don't have to just "push through." Workplace stress counselling can help you build resilience and find balance, even in demanding roles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does therapy for workplace stress work?

Yes, therapy is highly effective for managing workplace stress and burnout. We focus on practical strategies to set boundaries, manage overwhelm, and process work-related anxiety. Approaches like ACT help you detach from unhelpful thoughts and take action aligned with your professional and personal values.

Do you understand healthcare worker burnout?

Yes, I work with many healthcare professionals in Hamilton (Hamilton Health Sciences, St. Joe's). I understand the specific systemic pressures, compassion fatigue, and moral injury often present in these roles. We focus on sustainable coping strategies that acknowledge the realities of your working environment.

How much does therapy cost in Hamilton?

Sessions are $175 per 50-minute appointment. Most workplace insurance plans (including those for HHS, McMaster, and major industrial employers) cover Registered Psychotherapists. I provide detailed receipts for your claims.

Can I do sessions during my shift or lunch?

Absolutely. Virtual therapy offers the flexibility to meet during breaks or between shifts. All you need is a private space (like a car or private office) and an internet connection. This eliminates travel time and fits support into your actual workday.

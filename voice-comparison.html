My Recommendation

Use different blends for different pages:

  • Homepage hero, main service pages: 70/30 (trust + conversion)
  • About page, How I Work: 80/20 (maximum authenticity)
  • City/location pages, long-tail: 60/40 (SEO priority)
  • Blog, email, social: 80/20 or higher (pure voice)

The 70/30 is the sweet spot for most therapy pages—authentic enough to build trust, structured enough to convert and rank.

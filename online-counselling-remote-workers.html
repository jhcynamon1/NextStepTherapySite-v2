Frequently Asked Questions

How can online counselling help remote workers?
Online counselling helps remote workers manage isolation, set boundaries, prevent burnout, improve work-life balance, and develop healthy remote work habits.
Is therapy for remote workers covered by insurance?
Yes, most employee benefit plans and extended health insurance in Ontario cover psychotherapy services, including online counselling for remote workers.
When should remote workers seek counselling?
Consider counselling if you're experiencing isolation, difficulty disconnecting from work, burnout symptoms, anxiety about job security, or struggling with work-life boundaries.
Can I schedule sessions during work hours?
Absolutely. We offer flexible scheduling including lunch hour appointments. Many remote workers find midday sessions help reset their energy for the afternoon.
Will my employer know I'm in therapy?
No. Your therapy is completely confidential. We only share information with your explicit written consent, and insurance claims don't reveal session details to employers.

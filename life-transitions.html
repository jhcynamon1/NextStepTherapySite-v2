Frequently Asked Questions

How long does therapy for life transitions typically take?
The duration varies depending on the complexity of your transition and personal goals. Some clients benefit from short-term support (8-12 sessions), while others prefer longer-term guidance throughout their transition process.
Can therapy help even if I chose my transition?
Absolutely. Even positive, chosen transitions like marriage, career advancement, or moving can bring stress, uncertainty, and adjustment challenges. Therapy helps you navigate these changes smoothly and confidently.
What if I'm facing multiple transitions at once?
Multiple simultaneous transitions are common and can feel overwhelming. Therapy helps prioritize changes, manage stress, and develop strategies to handle multiple transitions without becoming overwhelmed.
Do you help with career changes specifically?
Yes, career transitions are a specialty. I help with career exploration, dealing with job loss, professional reinvention, workplace changes, and finding career direction that aligns with your values and lifestyle.
Is online therapy effective for life transitions?
Yes, online therapy is highly effective for transition support. Many clients appreciate the convenience and comfort of receiving guidance from their own space during already stressful periods of change.

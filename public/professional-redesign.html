How therapy works with me

Starting therapy can feel overwhelming. Here's what you can expect from our work together.

1

Free Consultation

We'll have a 20-minute conversation to discuss what you're looking for and see if we're a good fit. No pressure, no commitment.

2

Assessment & Goals

In our first session, we'll explore your current situation, discuss your goals, and create a treatment plan that works for you.

3

Regular Sessions

We'll meet weekly or bi-weekly (your choice) to work on your goals using evidence-based approaches tailored to your needs.

4

Progress & Growth

You'll develop practical tools, gain new insights, and see meaningful changes in how you think, feel, and handle life's challenges.

The Journey

My Therapeutic Process

A thoughtful, four-phase approach designed to create lasting transformation while honoring your unique circumstances and goals.

1

Deep Understanding

We explore how your challenges impact your daily experience, relationships, and overall wellbeing, identifying patterns and underlying dynamics with compassionate curiosity.

2

Pattern Recognition

Together we uncover patterns contributing to your concerns, understanding their development and function with evidence-based assessment tools.

3

Strategic Intervention

Develop personalized, research-backed strategies using advanced ACT and mindfulness approaches, tailored to your lifestyle, values, and specific goals.

4

Sustainable Integration

Create enduring change by weaving new insights and skills into your daily life, building long-term resilience and authentic wellbeing.

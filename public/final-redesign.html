Beyond Coping Strategies

I believe in helping you transform obstacles into opportunities for growth, not just managing symptoms. My approach combines clinical expertise with real-world understanding of life's pressures.

Advanced ACT Certification

Evidence-Based Approach

I specialize in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), combined with mindfulness-based interventions. This approach helps you develop psychological flexibility – the ability to stay present with your experiences and take action guided by your values, even when facing difficult thoughts and feelings.

My Background: With a Master's in Psychology and specialized training in crisis intervention, I understand how stress and anxiety impact your life. Having worked in high-pressure environments myself, I bring both clinical expertise and real-world experience to our work together.

What Makes This Different

Measure What Matters: I use validated measurement tools to track your progress and the quality of our therapeutic relationship. Brief assessments at the beginning and end of each session provide real-time data about your growth and our work together.

Personalized Process: Your therapy journey is unique. I adapt evidence-based approaches to fit your specific situation, goals, and life circumstances.

Lasting Change: Rather than just symptom management, we focus on building skills and insights that create sustainable improvement in how you think, feel, and navigate life's challenges.

My therapeutic process

Here's what you can expect when we work together, adapted to your unique needs and goals.

1

Insight & Understanding

We'll explore how your challenges specifically impact your daily life, relationships, and overall wellbeing, identifying patterns and triggers.

2

Pattern Recognition

Together we'll uncover the underlying patterns that contribute to your stress, anxiety, or other concerns, and understand how they developed.

3

Strategic Intervention

Develop practical, evidence-based strategies that fit your lifestyle and goals, using ACT and mindfulness approaches tailored to your situation.

4

Sustainable Integration

Create lasting change by integrating new skills and insights into your daily life, enhancing both your resilience and overall wellbeing.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How does virtual therapy Toronto work?

Virtual therapy sessions are conducted through secure, PIPEDA-compliant video platforms. You can access professional virtual therapy in Toronto and throughout Ontario with a stable internet connection. Research shows virtual therapy sessions are just as effective as in-person therapy for most concerns.

What is Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)?

ACT is an evidence-based therapy approach that helps you develop psychological flexibility. Rather than trying to eliminate difficult thoughts and feelings, ACT teaches you to accept them while taking action aligned with your values.

Does insurance cover therapy with a Registered Psychotherapist?

Most extended health plans in Ontario cover sessions with Registered Psychotherapists (CRPO). Coverage varies by plan, but typically ranges from $500-2000 annually. We provide detailed receipts for easy reimbursement. Read our complete guide to therapy costs and insurance in Ontario.

How quickly can I start therapy?

After your initial contact, we typically schedule a free 15-minute consultation within 2-3 days. If we're a good fit, first sessions are usually available within one week. Evening and weekend appointments available.

What is your therapeutic approach?

I use a structured approach grounded in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), with particular understanding of modern challenges like workplace stress, relationship patterns, and men's mental health. The focus is on building practical skills for managing high-stakes moments.

Are you accepting new clients?

Yes! We're currently accepting new clients across Ontario. Same-week appointments often available for urgent concerns. Evening and weekend time slots offered to accommodate busy professionals and students.

How much does a therapy session cost?

Individual therapy sessions are $160 per 50-minute session. Most clients use their extended health insurance benefits (which typically cover $500-$2,000 annually for therapy). We provide detailed receipts for easy insurance reimbursement. Payment accepted via credit card, debit, or e-transfer.

Common Questions

Real answers to the questions people ask about anxiety therapy

Why do I feel anxious all the time?

Constant anxiety often comes from your brain trying to protect you from perceived threats. Work stress, life changes, and overwhelming responsibilities can keep your nervous system in "high alert" mode. The good news? This is very treatable with the right therapeutic approach.

Can I really get help today?

Yes! Unlike many therapy services with long waiting lists, we offer same-day consultations and can often schedule your first full session within 24-48 hours. When you're struggling, waiting weeks for help isn't an option.

How does online therapy work?

We use secure, encrypted video calls that you can join from anywhere in Ontario. Many clients find online therapy more comfortable and convenient than in-person sessions. It's just as effective for anxiety and stress management.

Will my insurance cover this?

Most extended health plans in Ontario cover registered psychotherapy. We provide receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement. We can also help you check your coverage before your first session.

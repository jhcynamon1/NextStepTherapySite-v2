    General Questions

    Hi there! Have some questions about our  company? Find the answers below. 

    • What is Next Step Therapy?

      Next Step Therapy is a psychotherapy practice specializing in anxiety treatment and personal growth for professionals in Ontario. We provide virtual therapy sessions designed to support high achievers facing work stress, burnout, and performance anxiety.

    • Do you offer online therapy in Ontario?

      Yes. We provide secure virtual therapy sessions across Ontario, making it easy to access professional support from the comfort of your home or office.

    • How can therapy help with anxiety and burnout?

      Therapy offers evidence-based strategies to manage work-related anxiety, reduce stress, and prevent burnout. At Next Step Therapy, we focus on understanding the underlying patterns causing these challenges, not just managing symptoms. This approach creates lasting change that enhances both professional performance and personal wellbeing. Learn more about our Performance-Focused Therapy Services.

    • What therapy approaches do you use?

      We integrate evidence-based approaches such as:

      • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
      • Mindfulness-Based Techniques
      • Person-Centered Approaches
      • Integrative Methods

      These methods help clients gain clarity, develop resilience, and take meaningful action aligned with their values.

    • Who do you work with?

      Our therapy services are tailored for working professionals in medicine, law, finance, tech, and sports who want to manage anxiety, burnout, and perfectionism while maintaining their personal and career growth.

    • How do I book a therapy session?

      You can book a free consultation by clicking here or visiting our Contact page. Well discuss your needs and determine how our services can best support you.

    • Do you accept insurance for therapy?

      Yes. As a Registered Psychotherapist with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario (CRPO), many extended health insurance plans cover our services. We provide insurance receipts after each session that you can submit for reimbursement. Please check with your provider for coverage details.

    • How long does therapy take to see results?

      The timeframe varies, but many clients notice meaningful changes within a few sessions. Therapy is designed to help you build long-term strategies for managing stress, anxiety, and personal growth.

    • What is your cancellation policy?

      We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to accommodate other clients. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

    • Is therapy confidential?

      Yes. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our therapeutic relationship. We adhere strictly to confidentiality standards established by the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario and Ontario's Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA). Your privacy is our priority.

## üß† CRITICAL LEARNINGS: November 4, 2025

### 1. ‚ùå Content Length Misconception CORRECTED
**Previous Belief (WRONG):**
- Copied "Diesel Dudes" strategy (1,200 words)
- OR went opposite extreme (8,000 words like virtual-therapy-ontario)
- Thought shorter = more transactional

**New Understanding (CORRECT):**
- **Optimal range: 2,500-4,000 words** for therapy service pages
- Therapy searches are **"hybrid informational-transactional"** intent
- Unlike mechanics, therapy requires trust-building (more content than 1,200)
- Unlike pure informational, therapy seekers want action (less than 8,000)
- **Source:** Perplexity research + competitor analysis (Inner Oak, Brookhaven)

**Evidence:**
- Diesel Dudes (mechanics): 1,200 words works for "diesel repair" (pure transactional)
- Therapy competitors: 2,000-3,500 words (hybrid intent)
- Our 8,000-word pages: Too educational, dilutes focus
- Sweet spot: 2,500-4,000 words with clear CTAs

---

### 2. ‚ùå Therapy Search Intent Misunderstood
**Previous Belief (WRONG):**
- Therapy = same as mechanic/lawyer (transactional)
- Ahrefs says "informational" = wrong keyword target

**New Understanding (CORRECT):**
- Therapy searches are **hybrid informational-transactional**
- Users need education to build trust (informational component)
- But they ultimately want to book (transactional component)
- Ahrefs intent classification can be wrong - trust GSC data instead

**How This Changed Our Strategy:**
- Can't just copy Diesel Dudes' short transactional pages
- Need enough content for E-E-A-T and trust-building
- But focus on action-oriented CTAs throughout
- Balance education with conversion opportunities

---

### 4. ‚úÖ Keyword Cannibalization Detection Method

**Process We Used:**
1. **GSC Query Analysis:** Export top queries per page
2. **Overlap Identification:** Find queries appearing on multiple pages
3. **Volume Assessment:** Calculate total impressions being split
4. **Intent Mapping:** Determine if pages should target different intents
5. **Consolidation Decision:** Merge duplicates OR differentiate focus

**Burlington Example:**
- Query: "online therapy burlington" (2.4K volume)

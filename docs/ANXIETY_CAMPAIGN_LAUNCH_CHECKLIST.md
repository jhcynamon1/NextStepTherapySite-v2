## üîß TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** High CPC (>$8)
- **Solution:** Lower max CPC to $6, add more negative keywords, improve Quality Score

**Problem:** Low CTR (<2%)
- **Solution:** Rewrite ad headlines (more specific, emotional), test different offers

**Problem:** Zero conversions after 2 weeks
- **Solution:** Check GA4 tracking, test landing page on mobile, verify Jane link works

**Problem:** Budget exhausted early (before 8pm)
- **Solution:** Either increase daily budget OR lower bids to spread clicks throughout day

**Problem:** Quality Score <5
- **Solution:** Improve landing page relevance (add keyword to H1, H2), improve ad-to-page match

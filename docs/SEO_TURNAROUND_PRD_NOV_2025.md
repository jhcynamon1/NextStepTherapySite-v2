# Product Requirements Document (PRD): SEO Turnaround & Growth Engine
**Date:** November 2025

**Owner:** Jesse Cynamon (CRPO #10979)
**Status:** In Progress (Updated: Nov 18, 2025)

## 1. Executive Summary
Next Step Therapy (nextsteptherapy.ca) is currently underperforming in organic search with a **0.9% CTR** despite ~14,500 monthly impressions. High-intent keywords like "virtual therapy ontario" and "online therapy burlington" are generating thousands of impressions but zero clicks due to "invisible" titles and meta descriptions that fail to connect with the target audience.

This PRD outlines a 30-day aggressive turnaround plan to fix these critical leaks, capitalize on existing impressions, and position the practice as the premium choice for high-functioning professionals in Ontario.

## 2. Core Objectives & KPIs

### Primary Goal: Client Acquisition
*   **Increase Monthly Bookings:** Generate 5-10 new qualified client consultations per month via organic search within 60 days.

### SEO Performance KPIs (30-Day Targets)
*   **CTR Improvement:** Double site-wide CTR from 0.9% to **1.8%+**.
*   **Click Growth:** Increase monthly clicks from ~137 to **250+**.
*   **Ranking:** Move "Virtual Therapy Ontario" from Pos 30+ to **Top 10**.
*   **Conversion:** Capture first clicks/bookings for "Online Therapy Burlington" (currently 0/1,200).

## 3. Target Audience Persona
**The "High-Functioning" Professional**
*   **Demographics:** 25-45, Urban/Suburban Ontario (Toronto, Burlington, Ottawa), Income $80k+.
*   **Psychographics:** Looks successful on the outside but feels exhausted/anxious inside. Struggles with perfectionism, burnout, and "Sunday Night Anxiety."
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   "I don't have time for travel." (Needs virtual)
    *   "I need someone who 'gets' corporate pressure." (Needs relatable expert)
    *   "Is this covered by insurance?" (Needs financial clarity)
*   **Search Behavior:** Searches on mobile, late at night (Sunday/Monday), uses terms like "anxiety therapist near me," "burnout therapy," "adult adhd assessment."

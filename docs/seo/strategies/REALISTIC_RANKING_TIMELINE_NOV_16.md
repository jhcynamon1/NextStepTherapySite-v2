## ‚ö†Ô∏è BRUTAL HONESTY: WHAT COULD GO WRONG

### **Factors That Could DELAY Rankings:**

1. **YMYL (Your Money Your Life) Sites:**
   - Therapy = healthcare = Google scrutinizes heavily
   - E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) requirements higher
   - Could add 4-8 weeks to all estimates

2. **Domain Authority Limitations:**
   - If NextStepTherapy.ca has low DR (Domain Rating), rankings will be slower
   - Backlinks matter - do you have any quality local backlinks?

3. **Google Business Profile:**
   - Do you have verified GBP for these cities?
   - Without GBP, local pack rankings won't happen
   - Organic rankings still possible but slower

4. **Competition Underestimated:**
   - Keyword difficulty is one metric
   - Actual competition (established therapy practices) may be fiercer
   - Could add 50-100% to timeline

5. **Algorithm Updates:**
   - Google runs updates every few weeks
   - Healthcare site update could shuffle everything
   - Adds uncertainty to all estimates

6. **Indexing Issues:**
   - If Google doesn't fully index new sections, rankings delayed
   - Check GSC weekly to ensure pages stay indexed

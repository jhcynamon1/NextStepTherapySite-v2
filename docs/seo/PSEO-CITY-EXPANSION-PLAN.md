## Part 3: CRPO Compliance (CRITICAL)

### Mandatory Compliance Rules

**All content MUST adhere to CRPO (College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario) advertising standards.**

#### NEVER Include:
- [ ] ‚ùå Claims of "in-person" therapy (NextStep is VIRTUAL ONLY)
- [ ] ‚ùå Outcome guarantees ("You WILL feel better", "Cure your anxiety")
- [ ] ‚ùå Testimonials or client success stories
- [ ] ‚ùå Unverifiable superlatives ("Best therapist", "Most effective")
- [ ] ‚ùå Claims of specialization beyond credentials
- [ ] ‚ùå Pressure tactics or urgency ("Limited spots!")
- [ ] ‚ùå Comparisons to other therapists
- [ ] ‚ùå Medical claims (diagnosing, prescribing)

#### ALWAYS Include:
- [ ] ‚úÖ CRPO #10979 registration number
- [ ] ‚úÖ "Virtual therapy" or "Online therapy" (accurate service description)
- [ ] ‚úÖ Factual, verifiable statements only
- [ ] ‚úÖ Process-focused language ("We'll work on...", "Therapy can help...")
- [ ] ‚úÖ Clear that results vary by individual
- [ ] ‚úÖ Professional boundaries respected

#### Service Description Compliance:
```
CORRECT: "Virtual anxiety therapy across Ontario"
WRONG: "In-person and virtual therapy available"

CORRECT: "Therapy can help you develop coping strategies"
WRONG: "Therapy will cure your anxiety"

CORRECT: "CRPO-registered psychotherapist"
WRONG: "Ontario's best anxiety specialist"
```

## Part 4: Step-by-Step Production Workflow

### Step 1: City Research (10 minutes)

Gather this information before writing:

```
City: [Name]
Population: [Number]
Major Employers: [List 3-5 companies/industries]
Universities/Colleges: [If any]
Key Neighborhoods: [2-3 areas]
Commute Pattern: [To Toronto? Local? Highway?]
Local Stressors: [Cost of living, traffic, industry pressures]
Demographics: [Families, professionals, students, retirees?]
```

### Step 2: Content Generation (30 minutes)

Use therapy-content-generator skill:
- Provide city research context
- Request 1,500+ words
- Specify service type (anxiety/depression)
- Follow section structure from template

### Step 3: Voice Application (15 minutes)

Use brand-voice-therapy skill:
- Apply Jesse's voice markers
- Add vulnerability and check-ins
- Remove clinical/AI-sounding language
- Make conversational

### Step 4: Page Build (20 minutes)

1. Copy template from anxiety-therapy-burlington.html
2. Replace all content sections
3. Update hero headline and feature cards
4. Customize FAQ questions for city
5. Update meta title/description

### Step 5: Schema Implementation (10 minutes)

- Add MedicalWebPage JSON-LD
- Add FAQPage JSON-LD
- Verify with Google Rich Results Test

### Step 6: Quality Check (10 minutes)

Run through checklist in Part 3.

### Step 7: Internal Linking (5 minutes)

- Add city to main service page "Serving:" list
- Add to sitemap.xml
- Cross-link related city pages

**Total Time Per Page: ~1.5 hours**
**Total Time Per City (2 pages): ~2.5 hours** (research shared)

## Quick Reference

### Photo Tips
- Shoot during golden hour (morning or late afternoon)
- Remove clutter - minimalist is better
- Include plants/greenery if possible
- No client info visible (CRPO compliance)
- Landscape orientation (1200x900px minimum)

### Post Guidelines (CRPO-Compliant)
- **Do:** Educational content, availability updates, approach explanations
- **Don't:** Outcome promises, superlatives, fear appeals, testimonials

### Review Strategy (CRPO-Compliant)
- **Can ask:** Friends, family, colleagues (non-clients)
- **Cannot ask:** Current or former clients (CRPO 6.2.2 prohibits)

## üß† STRATEGIC LEARNINGS

### Content Length Discovery:
**Research Method:**
- Firecrawled 5 competitor pages
- Analyzed word count vs rankings
- Studied therapy vs mechanic search intent
- Consulted Perplexity AI for intent research

**Findings:**
- **Mechanics (Diesel Dudes):** 1,200 words (pure transactional)
- **Therapy (Inner Oak, Brookhaven):** 2,000-3,500 words (hybrid intent)
- **Our pages:** 8,000 words (too educational)
- **Sweet spot:** 2,500-4,000 words

**Why This Matters:**
- Therapy requires trust-building (more than 1,200 words)
- But users want action, not encyclopedia (less than 8,000 words)
- Balance education with conversion opportunities

---

### Multi-Service Hub Strategy:
**Learned from Inner Oak (Burlington #1 competitor):**
- Homepage = general hub
- Separate pages for EACH condition (anxiety, depression, ADHD, etc.)
- Each page = 500-800 words (shorter than expected!)
- Clear differentiation by condition

**Applied to Next Step Therapy:**
- ‚úÖ burlington-online-therapy.html = general hub (anxiety, depression, stress)
- ‚úÖ anxiety-therapy-burlington.html = anxiety-specific (panic, GAD, social anxiety)
- üéØ Next: depression-therapy-burlington.html (December)

## COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK: DOs AND DON'Ts

### CRPO Requirements (Critical for Ontario)

**ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED:**

‚ùå **Outcome Guarantees**
- "Guaranteed results," "cure your depression," "eliminate anxiety forever"

‚ùå **Testimonials**
- Cannot use client testimonials in ANY advertising
- Cannot request reviews from clients
- Cannot link ads to review sites

‚ùå **Comparative/Superlative Claims**
- "The best therapy," "Ontario's leading therapist," "better than others"

‚ùå **Fear-Based Appeals**
- "Without therapy your marriage will fail," "don't let anxiety destroy your life"

‚ùå **Personalized Health Claims** (Google Ads)
- "We know YOU'RE struggling," "YOUR depression needs treatment"

### APPROVED LANGUAGE:

‚úÖ **Credentials:** "Registered Psychotherapist (RP) | CRPO #10979"
‚úÖ **Services:** "Professional therapy for anxiety and depression across Ontario"
‚úÖ **Approach:** "Evidence-based ACT therapy with practical tools"
‚úÖ **CTAs:** "Book free 15-minute consultation," "Call to learn more"
‚úÖ **Benefits (not outcomes):** "Learn coping strategies," "develop tools for managing anxiety"

### Google Ads Restrictions:

**CANNOT USE:**
- Remarketing/retargeting (mental health = prohibited personalized advertising)
- Customer Match (uploading client lists)
- Health condition-based targeting
- Custom audiences based on site visits to therapy pages

**CAN USE:**
- Search ads targeting keywords
- Geographic targeting (Ontario, Toronto, cities)
- General demographic targeting (age 25-55)
- Contextual targeting (what they search, not who they are)

### Privacy & Tracking:

**Compliant Tracking:**
- ‚úÖ Conversion pixels on thank-you pages
- ‚úÖ Form submission tracking
- ‚úÖ Phone call tracking
- ‚ùå NO pixels on intake forms or health information pages
- ‚ùå NO remarketing tags

**Required:** Privacy policy disclosing tracking, cookie consent banner, PHIPA/PIPEDA compliance

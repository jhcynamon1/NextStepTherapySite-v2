## ‚ö° PRIORITY #1: CTR OPTIMIZATION (DECEMBER 1)

### Current Problem:
- **CTR: 0.06%** (1-2 clicks per month)
- **Industry Average: 2-3%**
- **Our Target: 3-5%**
- Burlington pages now target correct keywords (Nov 4 fix)
- But title tags don't compel clicks

### The Opportunity:
**With 2,316 impressions/month at 3% CTR = 69 clicks/month** (vs current 1-2)
- 35x improvement in traffic
- No additional ranking needed
- Pure conversion optimization

### Implementation Plan:

#### Step 1: Emotional Title Tag Transformation
**Current Formula:**
```
"[Service] Therapy [City] | [Details]"
```

**New Formula:**
```
"[Emotional Hook] | [Social Proof/Urgency] | [Location]"
```

**Examples:**

**anxiety-therapy-burlington.html:**
- ‚ùå Current: "Anxiety Therapy Burlington | Panic, Worry & Social Anxiety Treatment"
- ‚úÖ New: "Struggling with Panic Attacks in Burlington? Get Same-Week Support | CRPO #10979"

**burlington-online-therapy.html:**
- ‚ùå Current: "Online Therapy Burlington | Virtual Counselling & Mental Health Support"
- ‚úÖ New: "Burlington Online Therapy | Evening Appointments Available | 500+ Ontario Clients Helped"

#### Step 2: Meta Description Enhancement
**Add These Elements:**
1. **Specificity:** Mention exact conditions/symptoms
2. **Urgency:** "Same-week appointments" "Available this week"
3. **Social proof:** "500+ Ontario residents helped"
4. **Risk reversal:** "Free 15-min consultation"
5. **Insurance:** "Covered by most extended health plans"

#### Step 3: Testing Framework
**Baseline (Week 1 - Dec 1-7):**
- Deploy new titles/metas
- Record: CTR, impressions, clicks, position

**Monitor (Weeks 2-4 - Dec 8-31):**
- Week 2: Check if CTR improving
- Week 3: Identify best-performing titles
- Week 4: Document learnings for other pages

**Success Criteria:**
- CTR: 0.06% ‚Üí 2%+ (33x improvement minimum)
- Clicks: 1-2/month ‚Üí 40+/month
- Impressions: Maintain or increase (2,000-2,200)

### Files to Update:
1. anxiety-therapy-burlington.html (title + meta)
2. burlington-online-therapy.html (title + meta)
3. Other high-impression pages (if time permits)

**Reference Document:** PRD_CTR_OPTIMIZATION_DEC_1_2025.md

## Questions About $500/Month Budget?

**Q: Is $500 really enough?**
A: Yes. You'll get 15-25 conversions, which is statistically significant. Many therapy practices successfully test with $300-500/month.

**Q: Should I just wait until I have $2,000?**
A: No. Starting lean is smart. You can scale quickly if it works. The data from $500/month is almost as valuable as $2,000/month data.

**Q: What if I run out of budget before end of month?**
A: This is actually a good problem (means high traffic). You can either:
- Let it pause (you have enough data)
- Add another $250-500 mid-month
- Wait for Month 2 to scale

**Q: Can I start even lower, like $250/month?**
A: Technically yes, but not recommended. At $250/month:
- Only ~50 clicks/month (not enough for reliable data)
- May take 60-90 days to reach significance
- Budget spread too thin across 2 campaigns

$500/month is the practical minimum for 30-day decision-making.

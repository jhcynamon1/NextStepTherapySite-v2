### 8. CRPO Compliance: Red Lines in Ad Copy

**What You CANNOT Say (Prohibited by CRPO):**

‚ùå **Outcome Guarantees:**
- "Cure your anxiety"
- "Eliminate depression"
- "Fix your relationship"
- "Guaranteed results"
- "100% success rate"

‚ùå **Superlatives / Superiority Claims:**
- "Best therapist in Ontario"
- "Top-rated therapy"
- "Leading mental health clinic"
- "#1 therapist in Toronto"

‚ùå **Testimonials / Client Stories:**
- "Sarah says: 'This therapy changed my life!'"
- Direct client quotes
- Before/after stories
- Success case studies

‚ùå **Outcome-Based Pricing:**
- "Pay only if anxiety goes away"
- "Money-back guarantee"
- Results-based fee structures

‚ùå **Medical / Diagnostic Claims:**
- "Diagnose your ADHD"
- "Treat clinical depression"
- "Prescribe medication" (unless MD)

‚úÖ **Service Descriptions:**

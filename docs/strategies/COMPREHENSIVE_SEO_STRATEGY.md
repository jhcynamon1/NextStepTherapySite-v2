### üéØ Critical Learnings That Changed Our Strategy

#### 1. Content Length Optimization Discovery
**Previous Approach (WRONG):**
- Copied B2B mechanic model (Diesel Dudes) = 1,200 words (too short)
- OR created educational encyclopedia pages = 8,000 words (too long)

**Corrected Approach (VALIDATED):**
- **Optimal Range: 2,500-4,000 words** for therapy service pages
- **Why:** Therapy = hybrid informational-transactional intent
  - Users need education to build trust (unlike mechanics)
  - But they want to take action (unlike pure research)
- **Sources:** Perplexity AI research + Firecrawl competitor analysis (Inner Oak, Brookhaven)

#### 2. Therapy Search Intent Understanding
**Previous Misunderstanding:**
- Thought therapy searches = same as mechanic/lawyer (pure transactional)
- Believed Ahrefs "informational" label meant wrong keyword target

**Corrected Understanding:**
- Therapy searches are **"hybrid informational-transactional"**
- Users research symptoms, treatment options (informational)
- But ultimate goal is booking appointment (transactional)
- Need content that builds trust AND provides clear conversion paths

#### 3. Multi-Service Hub Strategy (Inner Oak Model)
**Discovery:** Burlington's #1 competitor (Inner Oak) uses:
- Homepage = general multi-service hub
- **Separate dedicated pages for EACH condition** (anxiety, depression, ADHD, etc.)
- Service pages = 500-800 words (shorter than expected!)
- Clear differentiation by mental health condition

**Applied to Next Step Therapy:**
- Created city-level multi-service hubs (burlington-online-therapy.html)
- Created condition-specific pages (anxiety-therapy-burlington.html)
- Link between them in hub-spoke structure
- Prevents keyword cannibalization

#### 4. Burlington Consolidation Case Study (Nov 4, 2025)

- 3 pages competing for overlapping keywords

## üõ°Ô∏è CRPO/PIPEDA Compliance Requirements (Competitive Advantage)

### Language Standards (vs Competitors Scoring 1-2/10)
- **Qualified Claims**: "Therapy may help with..." not "Therapy will cure..."
- **Professional Tone**: Clinical, evidence-based language
- **No Guarantees**: Avoid promises of specific outcomes
- **Individual Results**: Emphasize that results vary by person

### Required Disclaimers (Competitive Differentiator)
```
IMPORTANT: If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact:
- Crisis Services Canada: 1-833-456-4566
- Toronto Distress Centre: 416-408-4357
- Emergency Services: 911

This website provides general information about mental health services. 
It is not intended as medical advice. Please consult with a qualified 
healthcare professional for personalized treatment recommendations.
```

### Prohibited Content (Competitive Compliance Advantage)
- **No Testimonials**: Patient stories, reviews, or success stories
- **No Referral Fees**: Avoid language suggesting payment for referrals
- **No Medical Advice**: Don't diagnose or prescribe treatment
- **No Urgency Tactics**: Avoid "limited time" or pressure language

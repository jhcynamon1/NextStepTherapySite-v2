## Template Optimization Guidelines

### Conversion Rate Optimization
1. **Multiple CTAs:** Include consultation booking at hero, mid-page, and bottom
2. **Trust Signals:** Registration numbers, credentials, testimonials where appropriate  
3. **Social Proof:** Statistics, research citations, success indicators
4. **Urgency/Scarcity:** Limited consultation slots, same-week availability
5. **Risk Reversal:** Free consultation, no-pressure approach

### SEO Optimization
1. **Keyword Density:** 1-2% for primary keywords, natural integration
2. **Semantic Keywords:** Include related terms and synonyms naturally
3. **Local SEO:** City/province references, local statistics
4. **Internal Linking:** Connect to related therapy specialties
5. **External Authority:** Link to research studies and professional organizations

### Mobile Optimization
1. **Responsive Design:** Templates adapt to all screen sizes
2. **Touch-Friendly:** Large buttons, easy navigation
3. **Fast Loading:** Optimized images, minimal scripts
4. **Readable Text:** Appropriate font sizes, good contrast
5. **Simple Navigation:** Easy access to booking and contact

These templates provide the foundation for rapid scaling across multiple therapy niches while maintaining high conversion rates and SEO performance.

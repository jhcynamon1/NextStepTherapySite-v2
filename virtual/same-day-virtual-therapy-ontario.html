Same Day Therapy Questions

Is same day therapy as effective as regular therapy?

Yes, the therapy itself is identical—just available sooner. Same professional standards, evidence-based approaches, and therapeutic relationship. The only difference is you don't have to wait.

What if I need ongoing support after today?

Same day sessions can transition to regular therapy if needed. We'll discuss follow-up options during your session. Many clients continue with weekly or biweekly support. Today is just the beginning.

How do I know if I need same day vs. emergency services?

If you're safe but struggling, same day therapy is appropriate. If there's immediate danger, use crisis services. When you call, we can help determine the right level of care. Your safety is always priority.

Can I get same day therapy anywhere in Ontario?

Yes! Virtual therapy means same day access throughout Ontario. From Windsor to Kenora, Toronto to Timmins. Your location doesn't limit your access to immediate support.

What if no same day slots are available?

We prioritize urgent needs and maintain flexibility for same day requests. If today isn't possible, we'll offer the very next available slot. Often within 24-48 hours maximum.

Is same day therapy covered by insurance?

Same insurance coverage as regular therapy sessions. We provide receipts for reimbursement. Your insurance doesn't distinguish between same day and scheduled sessions.

Do I need to explain why it's urgent?

No detailed explanation required. Simply say you need same day support. We trust your assessment of your needs. The session will address what's most urgent for you.

How Virtual Therapy Ontario Sessions Work

Starting virtual therapy feels less intimidating when you know what to expect. We've made the process as simple and comfortable as possible.

Getting Started is Simple:

1. Book Your Free Consultation

A 15-minute call to discuss your needs and answer questions. No pressure, just conversation about whether we're a good fit. We'll also do a quick tech check to ensure everything works smoothly.

2. Set Up Your Space

Find a private, comfortable spot in your home. Could be your bedroom, home office, even your car in the driveway. Anywhere you feel safe to talk openly. Good lighting helps but isn't essential. Headphones optional but can increase privacy.

3. Join Your Session

Click the secure link sent to your email. No downloads or complicated software. Works on any device—computer, tablet, or phone. If technology fails, we switch to phone instantly. Your session won't be interrupted.

4. Connect Authentically

After a few minutes, you'll forget it's virtual. The therapeutic relationship builds naturally. Many clients report feeling more open from the comfort of their own space. The work we do together is real, regardless of the medium.

Virtual Therapy Questions Answered

What technology do I need for virtual therapy?

Any device with internet and a camera—computer, tablet, or smartphone. The platform works through your web browser, no downloads required. Reliable internet helps but even moderate speeds work fine.

How long are virtual therapy sessions?

Standard sessions are 50 minutes, same as in-person. This allows time to settle in, work through topics, and wrap up thoughtfully. Longer sessions available if needed.

What if there's an emergency?

Clear emergency protocols are established in first session. Local crisis resources are always available. We'll identify your nearest emergency services. Safety plans created collaboratively when needed.

Can I do therapy from my car or office?

Absolutely. Many clients connect from cars for privacy. Office sessions during lunch breaks are common. Anywhere you feel comfortable and private works. We adapt to your circumstances.

Is virtual therapy regulated in Ontario?

Yes, virtual therapy follows the same CRPO regulations as in-person. Same ethical standards and professional guidelines apply. Your protection and rights remain identical.

What areas of Ontario do you serve?

All of Ontario! From Windsor to Kenora, Toronto to Timmins. If you're in Ontario, virtual therapy is available to you. No geographic limitations within the province.

How quickly can I start virtual therapy?

Often within the same week you reach out. Free consultation can happen within 24-48 hours. First full session shortly after. No waitlists for virtual appointments.

## 2. Ontario Therapist Consolidation Strategy

### The Problem: Keyword Cannibalization

You currently have **4 overlapping provincial pages** targeting similar "find a therapist in Ontario" intent:
1. `/counselling-ontario.html`
2. `/counsellor-ontario.html`
3. `/mental-health-therapist-ontario.html`
4. `/therapy-near-me-ontario.html`

**Why This Is a Problem:**
- **Keyword cannibalization:** All 4 pages compete for the same search queries, diluting authority
- **Unwinnable SERP:** "Therapist ontario" (SV 150, KD 18) is dominated by directories (Psychology Today, OSRP, CRPO)
- **Directory-seeking intent:** Users searching "therapist ontario" want a search tool, not an individual practice
- **Wasted crawl budget:** Google is indexing 4 similar pages instead of consolidating authority

### SERP Analysis: "Therapist Ontario" (SV 150, KD 18)

**Top 10 Results:**
1. Psychology Today - Find Therapists directory
2. OAMHP - Ontario Association of Mental Health Professionals directory
3. OSRP - Ontario Society of Registered Psychotherapists directory
4. CRPO - College of Registered Psychotherapists (regulatory body)
5. Ontario Psychological Association - Find a Psychologist directory
6. ontario.ca - Government mental health resources
7. Affordable Therapy Network - Low-cost directory
8. Reddit - "How do I get a therapist in Ontario?" (60+ comments)
9. First Session - How To Find a Therapist guide + directory
10. Psychotherapy Matters - Find A Mental Health Care Provider directory

**Key Insight:** **7 of 10 results are directories.** Individual practices cannot rank for this query because users want search tools, not individual providers.

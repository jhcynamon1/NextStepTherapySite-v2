What Depression Actually Feels Like

Maybe you're exhausted in a way that sleep doesn't fix. You wake up tired. You go through your day tired. Things that used to matterâ€”hobbies, friends, goalsâ€”don't seem to matter anymore. You're functioning, but something's off.

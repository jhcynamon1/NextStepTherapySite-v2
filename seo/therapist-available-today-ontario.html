Questions About Same-Day Therapy

How quickly can I actually get a session?

Often within hours of contacting us, depending on availability. We prioritize urgent requests and do our best to accommodate same-day needs. Call rather than email for fastest response.

Is same-day therapy as effective as regular therapy?

Yes. While ongoing therapy allows for deeper work, same-day sessions provide crucial support when you need it most. Many clients start with urgent sessions and continue with regular therapy.

What if I'm not in crisis but just realized I need help?

That's perfectly valid. The moment you recognize you need support is the right time to seek it. You don't need to be in crisis to deserve immediate care.

Can I book same-day sessions regularly?

While we accommodate urgent needs, we also offer regular scheduled sessions. Many clients transition from same-day support to consistent weekly appointments.

What technology do I need for virtual therapy?

Just a device with internet, camera, and microphone - phone, tablet, or computer works. We use secure, PHIPA-compliant platforms that work on any device.

Is virtual therapy private and confidential?

Absolutely. We use encrypted, secure platforms. Your privacy is protected by professional standards and Ontario privacy laws.

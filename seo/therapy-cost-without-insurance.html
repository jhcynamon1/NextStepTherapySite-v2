Standard Therapy Cost in Ontario

$175 per 50-minute session - This is the standard rate for individual therapy sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist in Ontario.

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. What you see is what you pay.

Making Therapy Affordable

I understand that therapy is an investment, and not everyone has the same financial circumstances. That's why I offer several options to make mental health support accessible:

Payment Plans

Break down session costs into manageable payments. We can arrange weekly or bi-weekly payment schedules that work with your budget.

E-transfer & Credit Cards

Flexible payment methods including e-transfer, credit cards, and direct payment options to make transactions convenient.

Virtual Sessions

Save on travel costs and time with secure virtual therapy sessions. Same quality care, more convenient and affordable.

Sliding Scale (Limited)

A few reduced-rate spots available for students, seniors, or those experiencing financial hardship. Contact to discuss options.

What You Get for $175

Your investment in therapy includes:

  • 50 minutes of focused, individual attention from a CRPO-registered psychotherapist
  • Evidence-based therapy techniques including Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific goals and challenges
  • Between-session support via email for urgent questions or clarifications
  • Professional expertise in anxiety, depression, workplace stress, and life transitions
  • Secure, confidential environment both in-person and virtually

Compare Your Options

When considering therapy costs, it's worth comparing different types of mental health support:

Private Practice Therapists

$150-$200/session
Immediate availability, specialized expertise, personalized care

Community Mental Health

Free - $50/session
Long wait lists (3-6 months), limited sessions, group-focused

Employee Assistance Programs

Free (if available)
Usually 3-6 sessions max, basic support, workplace conflicts of interest

Online Therapy Platforms

$80-$120/session
Limited regulation, varying quality, less personalized

When Therapy Becomes Worth Every Dollar

  • Improved work performance and career advancement opportunities
  • Better relationships leading to less conflict and stress
  • Reduced need for other mental health interventions
  • Decreased sick days and healthcare costs
  • Enhanced quality of life and personal satisfaction
